What We Offer

Management

With the information automatically collected from your POS system, you have instant access to performance and metrics such as net sales by hour, employee working hours and inventory levels. NCR’s reporting tools help you discover the levers you need to pull so you can increase your profitability and grow your business. Without having to do the legwork of manually managing your employees, inventory, and sales performance, you can spend more time connecting with your employees, focusing on the customer experience and growing your business.

Services

F & B Management will help you design, implement, reconfigure, upgrade and/or enhance your POS solution. Our proven processes and industry experts also ensure that you get a faster install, fewer issues and a high quality solution that’s easier and less costly to maintain over time. NCR’s full suite of easy to use products are everything you need in a technology solution point-of-sale, and can include order confirmation displays, loyalty, gift cards, back office and enterprise reporting-in an integrated package.

Operations

We apply our in-depth knowledge of restaurants and technology to provide a single point of accountability and innovative processes to unlock creative answers to customer problems. Our approach increases user satisfaction, maximizes cost savings and enables you to focus on your core business. NCR products have a competitive acquisition cost. We are able to provide a lower overall ongoing cost over a 5-7 year period. As a result, our trained professionals can show you how our solutions can provide a return on your investment which adds to your bottom line.

Marketing

F&B Management offers flexible hardware options, including handheld devices, POS terminals and customer displays. Our hardware features NCR Aloha POS software which has the capability of many add-on enhancements such as loyalty programs, back office management, security and more. NCR has one of the industry’s most reliable solutions with maximum uptime and redundancy.

Back Office

NCR’s applications give you unprecedented control over your IT operations by providing real-time visibility into your software and hardware. Troubleshoot and resolve issues rapidly before they impact your operations and configure your system for optimal use.

Support

Our Support Services provide you with 24/7/365 days a week. We can also provide local hardware and software support so you’re up and running as soon as possible. With robust reporting and analysis and flexible service level agreements, we are focused everyday on delivering exceptional service and meeting the needs of our customers.

Software

Our multi-functional restaurant POS solution, ALOHA, gives operators all the tools they need to boost sales and increase the pace of service. Staff can use ALOHA to enter orders and payments, streamline food preparation and delivery, and improve operations. NCR Aloha POS is ideal for quick service, fast casual, casual dining and fine dining restaurants.

Marketing

As a restaurant operator in the digital age, it’s important for you to connect with your customers to market your establishment. By doing so, you can reward your repeat guests, while learning how they felt about their dining experience, and find ways to improve to appeal to more customers and drive new business. F & B has a number of NCR solutions to help you reach out to customers as well as measure their response.
